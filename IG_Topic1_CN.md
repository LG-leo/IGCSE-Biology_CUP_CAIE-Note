### ⚠️ 三、Paper 2 常见陷阱（基于真题失分点）

| 陷阱 | 正确理解 | 错误选项特征 | 真题出处 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Respiration = breathing | Respiration is chemical reactions in cells, not breathing | 选项中用 "breathing" 或 "gas exchange" 代替 "chemical reactions" | 2021 0610/22; 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
| Excretion = egestion | Excretion removes metabolic waste (urea, CO₂); egestion removes undigested food (faeces) | 选项中把 "removal of faeces" 定义为 excretion | 2021 0610/22; 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
| Growth = increase in wet mass | Growth = permanent increase in dry mass | 选项中用 "increase in mass" 而不提 "dry" 或 "permanent" | 2021 0610/22; 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
| Viruses are prokaryotes | Viruses are not classified in any kingdom; not considered living | 选项中把 virus 放在 Prokaryote 或任何其他界 | 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
| Fungi have cellulose cell walls | Fungi have cell walls made of chitin | 选项中写 "cellulose" | 2021 0610/22; 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
| Bacteria have a true nucleus | Bacteria (prokaryotes) have no true nucleus; DNA is circular and free in cytoplasm | 选项中写 "has a nucleus" | 2021 0610/22; 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
| Binomial name written as *Homo Sapiens* | Genus capitalised, species lower case: *Homo sapiens* (italicised or underlined) | 选项中写 "Homo Sapiens" (species capitalised) 或未斜体 | 2021 0610/22; 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
| Mule is a species | Horse and donkey are different species because mule is infertile | 选项中写 "horse and donkey are the same species" | 2021 0610/22; 2022 0610/22; 2023 0610/22; 2024 0610/22 |
