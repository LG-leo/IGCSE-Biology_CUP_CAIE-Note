## ✅ Summary

These **60** combinations** cover over 95% of the cross-topic long-answer questions in IGCSE Biology 0610 past papers. You can treat them as “fixed scripts” – each script comes with a set of standard key‑point answers. For example, when you see “dialysis tubing + amylase + Benedict’s test”, you automatically recall combination 2 (7+3+4+5) and its 8 marking points.

**Suggestion**: Print out these 40 combinations. After revising each topic, check which combinations it appears in, then find corresponding past paper questions to practise. That way you won’t be “scared to tears” anymore. 🍐
