### 📌 一、Paper 2 – 高频考点与答题关键词 (选择题)

| 考点类别 | 真题关键词 (Question Keyword) | 答题关键词 (Answer Key) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **无性生殖定义** | 以下哪项是对无性生殖的准确定义？ | A process resulting in genetically identical offspring from one parent. |
| **无性生殖 vs 有性生殖** | 无性生殖与有性生殖的主要区别是什么？ | 无性生殖：一个亲本，无配子融合，后代基因相同；有性生殖：两个亲本，配子融合，后代有遗传变异。 |
| **无性生殖例子识别** | 以下哪个是无性生殖的例子？ | 细菌二分裂 / 马铃薯块茎发芽 / 草莓匍匐茎。 |
| **细菌分裂类型** | 细菌通过哪种细胞分裂进行无性生殖？ | Binary fission (二分裂)。 |
| **有性生殖定义** | 以下哪项是有性生殖的定义？ | A process involving the fusion of the nuclei of two gametes to form a zygote, producing offspring that are genetically different. |
| **配子特征** | 配子（gametes）的主要特征是什么？ | Haploid (单倍体); contain half the number of chromosomes. |
| **配子融合产物** | 配子融合后形成什么？ | Zygote (合子) – diploid (二倍体)。 |
| **精子的适应特征** | 精子有哪些适应特征？ | Flagellum (tail) for swimming; acrosome contains enzymes to penetrate egg; many mitochondria for energy. |
| **卵细胞的适应特征** | 卵细胞有哪些适应特征？ | Contains energy stores for early development; jelly coat changes after fertilisation to prevent polyspermy. |
| **精子和卵细胞比较** | 比较精子和卵细胞的大小、数量、结构和运动性。 | **Sperm**: very small, numerous, streamlined with tail, motile. **Egg**: large, few (one per cycle), round, non-motile. |
| **受精发生部位** | 受精发生在女性生殖系统的哪个部位？ | Oviduct (输卵管)。 |
| **着床部位** | 受精卵（胚泡）植入（着床）的部位是哪里？ | Uterus (子宫)。 |
| **胎盘功能** | 胎盘的主要功能是什么？ | Allows exchange of dissolved nutrients, gases (O₂ and CO₂), and excretory products (urea) between mother and fetus; also produces hormones and provides immune protection. |
| **胚泡** | 受精后形成的一团细胞叫什么？ | Embryo (早期称 zygote，发育到囊胚阶段植入子宫)。 |
| **羊水功能** | 羊水的主要功能是什么？ | Protects the fetus from mechanical damage / acts as a shock absorber. |
| **月经周期激素** | 控制月经周期的四种主要激素是什么？ | FSH, LH, oestrogen, progesterone. |
| **排卵** | 排卵 (ovulation) 发生在月经周期的哪一天？ | Day 14 (of a typical 28-day cycle). |
| **FSH 功能** | FSH 的主要功能是什么？ | Stimulates egg maturation in the ovary and stimulates oestrogen production. |
| **LH 功能** | LH 的主要功能是什么？ | Triggers ovulation (release of egg) and stimulates progesterone production. |
| **孕酮 (Progesterone)** | 孕酮的主要功能是什么？ | Maintains the uterus lining (thickening) for implantation; if no fertilisation, levels drop and menstruation occurs. |
| **青春期激素** | 青春期引起第二性征发育的主要激素是什么？ | Testosterone (in males) and oestrogen (in females). |
| **HIV 传播方式** | HIV 可以通过哪些体液传播？ | Blood, semen, vaginal fluids, breast milk. |
| **STI 控制** | 如何控制性传播感染 (STI) 的传播？ | Abstinence; using condoms; screening blood donations; education; treating infected individuals. |
| **花粉粒 (虫媒 vs 风媒)** | 虫媒花和风媒花的花粉粒有什么区别？ | 虫媒：较大、有粘性/刺状；风媒：小、光滑、轻、量大。 |
| **花的结构与功能配对** | 将花的各部分与其功能配对。 | sepal: protects flower bud; petal: attracts insects; anther: produces pollen; filament: supports anther; stigma: receives pollen; style: connects stigma to ovary; ovary: contains ovules; ovule: contains female gamete. |
| **种子萌发所需条件** | 种子萌发需要哪些环境条件？ | Water, oxygen, and a suitable temperature. |
| **种子萌发条件选择** | 种子萌发必须始终具备的环境条件是哪个？ | Water and oxygen (光照并非所有种子都需要)。 |

### 📝 二、Paper 4 – 典型题目与答题模板 (理论题)

| 题型 | 真题关键词 | 答题模板 (按评分标准) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **无性生殖定义与优缺点** | (a) Define asexual reproduction. (b) State two advantages of asexual reproduction in the wild. | (a) Asexual reproduction is the production of genetically identical offspring from a single parent. (b) 1. Rapid population growth when conditions are favourable. 2. Only one parent required (more time and energy efficient). 3. Allows organisms to exploit suitable environments quickly. |
| **有性生殖定义与优缺点** | Define sexual reproduction and state two disadvantages of sexual reproduction in crop plants. | Sexual reproduction is the fusion of the nuclei of two gametes to form a zygote, producing offspring that are genetically different. Disadvantages in crop plants: 1. Variation may lead to less successful offspring. 2. Requires pollination (may fail if pollinators absent). 3. Slower than asexual reproduction. |
| **受精与着床过程** | Describe the process of fertilisation and implantation in humans. | Fertilisation: the fusion of the nuclei of a sperm and an egg cell occurs in the oviduct, forming a zygote (diploid). Implantation: the zygote divides by mitosis to form a ball of cells (embryo / blastocyst). The embryo implants into the lining of the uterus (endometrium). |
| **胎盘的结构与功能** | Describe the function of the placenta in humans. | The placenta is the organ through which the mother and fetus exchange materials. It allows the exchange of dissolved nutrients (e.g. glucose, amino acids), gases (oxygen and carbon dioxide), and excretory products (urea) between the blood of the mother and the blood of the fetus. It also acts as a barrier to some pathogens and produces hormones (e.g. progesterone). |
| **月经周期激素调节** | Explain how FSH, LH, oestrogen and progesterone control the menstrual cycle. | **FSH**: released by pituitary gland, causes egg to mature in ovary and stimulates oestrogen production. **Oestrogen**: produced by ovary, causes repair and thickening of uterus lining, inhibits FSH production, and stimulates LH release. **LH**: released by pituitary gland, triggers ovulation and stimulates progesterone production. **Progesterone**: produced by ovary (corpus luteum), maintains uterus lining. If no fertilisation, progesterone levels drop → uterus lining breaks down (menstruation). |
| **花粉管生长与受精 (Supplement)** | Describe the growth of the pollen tube and fertilisation in plants. | After pollination, the pollen grain absorbs nutrients and germinates, growing a pollen tube down through the style. The pollen tube enters the ovule through the micropyle. The male gamete (pollen nucleus) fuses with the female gamete (egg nucleus) in the ovule – this is fertilisation. The fertilised ovule becomes a seed; the ovary becomes a fruit. |
| **自花传粉 vs 异花传粉 (Supplement)** | Distinguish between self-pollination and cross-pollination. | **Self-pollination**: transfer of pollen from anther to stigma of the same flower or a different flower on the same plant. **Cross-pollination**: transfer of pollen from anther of one flower to stigma of a flower on a different plant of the same species. Cross-pollination increases genetic variation; self-pollination ensures reproduction without pollinators. |
| **种子萌发实验设计** | Describe how you would investigate the conditions required for seed germination. | Set up four test tubes with 10 seeds each. Tube 1: water + oxygen (air) + room temperature – control (expected to germinate). Tube 2: no water (dry cotton wool), oxygen + room temp. Tube 3: water + no oxygen (boiled water covered with oil layer) + room temp. Tube 4: water + oxygen + low temperature (place in refrigerator). Count number of germinated seeds after 5-7 days. |
| **HIV 感染与 AIDS** | Explain how HIV can lead to AIDS. | HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) is a pathogen that infects and destroys lymphocytes (white blood cells). As the number of lymphocytes decreases, the immune system becomes weakened. This leads to AIDS (Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome), where the body cannot fight off opportunistic infections. |
| **胎盘屏障 (Supplement)** | Explain why some pathogens and toxins can cross the placenta while others cannot. | The placenta acts as a barrier, but it is not completely impermeable. Some pathogens (e.g. HIV, rubella virus) and toxins (e.g. alcohol, nicotine) are small enough or have mechanisms to cross the placental barrier, potentially causing harm or developmental problems to the fetus. |

### ⚠️ 三、Paper 2 常见陷阱（基于真题失分点）

| 陷阱 | 正确理解 | 错误选项特征 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **混淆受精和着床部位** | 受精发生在**输卵管 (oviduct)**，着床发生在**子宫 (uterus)**。 | 选项中写“fertilisation occurs in the uterus”或“implantation occurs in the oviduct”。 |
| **混淆胎盘和脐带功能** | **胎盘**负责母体与胎儿间的物质交换；**脐带**是连接胎儿和胎盘的血管通道。 | 选项中写“umbilical cord exchanges nutrients”。 |
| **混淆无性生殖和有性生殖** | 无性生殖：一个亲本，无配子，后代基因相同；有性生殖：两个亲本，配子融合，后代有变异。 | 选项中写“asexual reproduction involves gametes”。 |
| **混淆精子与卵细胞的特征** | 精子：小、有尾、能动、数量多；卵细胞：大、无尾、不能动、数量少。 | 选项中写“sperm are large”或“eggs are motile”。 |
| **混淆 FSH 和 LH 功能** | **FSH** → 卵泡发育和雌激素分泌；**LH** → 触发排卵和黄体酮分泌。 | 选项中写“LH causes egg maturation”或“FSH triggers ovulation”。 |
| **混淆孕酮和雌激素的作用** | **雌激素**：修复和增厚子宫内膜；**孕酮**：维持子宫内膜。 | 选项中写“oestrogen maintains the uterus lining”。 |
| **混淆风媒花和虫媒花的特征** | 虫媒：花瓣大、颜色鲜艳、有香味、花粉大且有粘性；风媒：花瓣小或不明显、无香味、花粉小且轻、量大。 | 选项中交换两者的特征。 |
| **混淆自花传粉和异花传粉的后果** | 自花传粉 → 减少变异；异花传粉 → 增加变异。 | 选项中写“self-pollination increases genetic variation”。 |
| **错误认为抗生素能治疗 HIV** | HIV 是病毒，抗生素对病毒无效。 | 选项中写“HIV can be treated with antibiotics”。 |
| **错误认为羊水提供营养** | 羊水主要起保护作用（缓冲机械冲击），不提供营养。营养通过胎盘从母体血液获得。 | 选项中写“amniotic fluid provides nutrients to the fetus”。 |

### 📌 五、单点考题示例 (Single Examination Point Questions)

| 单点考题 | 答案 |
| :--- | :--- |
| Define asexual reproduction. | The production of genetically identical offspring from one parent. |
| Name one example of asexual reproduction. | Binary fission in bacteria / runners in strawberry plants / tubers in potato plants. |
| Define sexual reproduction. | The fusion of the nuclei of two gametes to form a zygote, producing offspring that are genetically different. |
| What is fertilisation? | The fusion of the nuclei of a male gamete and a female gamete to form a zygote. |
| Name the cell structure where the acrosome is found. | Sperm cell (head region). |
| Name the tube that carries sperm from the testis to the urethra. | Sperm duct. |
| Name the organ that produces testosterone. | Testis / testes. |
| Name the hormone that causes an egg to mature in the ovary. | FSH (Follicle-Stimulating Hormone). |
| Name the hormone that triggers ovulation. | LH (Luteinising Hormone). |
| Name the hormone that maintains the uterus lining during pregnancy. | Progesterone. |
| Name the structure that connects the fetus to the placenta. | Umbilical cord. |
| Name the fluid-filled sac that surrounds and protects the fetus. | Amniotic sac / amnion. |
| Name one method of transmission of HIV. | Sexual contact / contact with infected blood / mother to child (during pregnancy, birth, or breast‑feeding). |
| Name one way to control the spread of STIs. | Using condoms / abstinence / education / screening blood donations. |
| Name two conditions required for seed germination (other than a suitable temperature). | Water and oxygen. |
| Name the part of the flower that develops into a fruit after fertilisation. | Ovary. |
| Name the part of the flower that develops into a seed after fertilisation. | Ovule. |
